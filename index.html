Research peptides are used in scientific and academic laboratory research settings. They are utilized to study various biological processes, receptor interactions, and cellular mechanisms. Our COAs include HPLC purity data, mass spectrometry confirmation, and appearance/solubility testing. A COA is included with every order at no additional charge.
