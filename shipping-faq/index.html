Shipping, payment & returns

The logistics.

For anything not covered here, email support@titanpeptidelab.com. Replies usually arrive inside 24–48 hours.

01

Shipping

Dispatched from Reno within 24 to 48 hours of payment confirmation. Clean outer carton, insulated inner pack, and cold-chain handling where liquids require it.

01How fast does an order leave the laboratory?+
Orders confirmed before 14:00 PT usually move same day. Everything else leaves inside 48 hours. Tracking is sent as soon as the dispatch record is closed.
02What does the package look like?+
A plain outer carton with a neutral return label. Inside are the product, packing protection, printed certificate, and temperature-conscious handling where needed.
03Which products ship cold?+
Nasal sprays and other liquid formats receive cold-conscious packing. Lyophilized powder generally ships ambient.
04Do you ship internationally?+
Yes. International orders are handled with express carriers and the buyer remains responsible for local import rules.
05What about tracking?+
Domestic orders use USPS Priority or UPS Ground. International orders use DHL Express when applicable. If tracking does not move within 48 hours, contact support.

03

Returns

Because the products are sold for research use only, the returns policy is built around quality incidents and shipment issues, not casual restocking.

01What is your returns policy?+
If a product arrives broken, mislabeled, or fails visual inspection, email support within 48 hours with photos and the order can be replaced.
02What if the certificate does not match the bottle?+
Hold the product and email QA immediately. That is treated as a quality incident and reviewed urgently.
03What if the peptide fails my own in-house assay?+
Send the chromatogram and method. Titan will compare against the retained sample and review whether a replacement batch is warranted.
04What if the package is lost?+
If tracking shows delivered but the package is missing, open a carrier claim and copy support@titanpeptidelab.com so the file can be tracked on our side too.
