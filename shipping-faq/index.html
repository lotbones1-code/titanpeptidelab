§S — Shipping, Payment, Returns

The logistics.

For anything not covered here, email support@titanpeptidelab.com. Replies usually arrive inside 24 to 48 hours, with destination-specific help for buyers inside and outside America.

§S1

Shipping

Dispatched from Reno after payment confirmation. Global buyers are welcome: clean outer carton, insulated inner pack, and region-aware handling for domestic and cross-border orders.

01How fast does an order leave the laboratory?+
Orders confirmed before 14:00 PT usually move same day. Everything else leaves inside 48 hours. Tracking is sent as soon as the dispatch record is closed, whether the destination is domestic or international.
02What does the package look like?+
A plain outer carton with a neutral return label. Inside are the product, packing protection, printed certificate, and temperature-conscious handling where needed.
03Which products ship cold?+
Nasal sprays and other liquid formats receive cold-conscious packing. Lyophilized powder generally ships ambient.
04Do you ship internationally?+
Yes. Buyers worldwide can place an order request. If a tracked carrier lane is available, Titan will prepare the shipment; the buyer remains responsible for local import rules, customs, VAT, brokerage, and destination restrictions.
05What about tracking?+
Domestic orders use USPS Priority or UPS Ground. International orders use DHL Express or the cleanest available tracked lane for the destination. If tracking does not move within 48 hours, contact support.
§S2

Payment

Crypto-first payment guidance for domestic and international buyers, with faster confirmation once payment is received on the selected chain.

01Which cryptocurrencies do you accept?+
Live checkout supports BTC, ETH, USDC ERC-20, SOL, and USDC SPL. If you need another major crypto rail, contact Titan before sending so the correct receiving address can be confirmed first.
02Why crypto only?+
It clears quickly, works across borders, removes card processor instability from the category, and keeps fulfillment tied to a confirmed on-chain transaction.
03Can I pay by card?+
No. Checkout is crypto-only at the moment.
04What discount codes are active right now?+
FIRST10 for first orders, BULK15 for 3 or more items, TITAN20 above $250, and VIP25 on the third repeat order. Codes do not stack, and free-shipping thresholds vary by destination region.
05Is there a volume contract for laboratories?+
Yes. Email qa@titanpeptidelab.com with compound list, quantity, and cadence for a standing quote.
§S3

Returns

Because the products are sold for research use only, the returns policy is built around quality incidents, shipment issues, and cross-border delivery clarity, not casual restocking.

01What is your returns policy?+
If a product arrives broken, mislabeled, or fails visual inspection, email support within 48 hours with photos and the order can be replaced.
02What if the certificate does not match the bottle?+
Hold the product and email QA immediately. That is treated as a quality incident and reviewed urgently.
03What if the peptide fails my own in-house assay?+
Send the chromatogram and method. Titan will compare against the retained sample and review whether a replacement batch is warranted.
04What if the package is lost?+
If tracking shows delivered but the package is missing, open a carrier claim and copy support@titanpeptidelab.com so the file can be tracked on our side too.
