Quality & lot transparency

01How is purity verified?+
Every batch is HPLC tested in-house, then sent to an independent ISO 17025 lab for retest. Release target is ≥99% purity. The in-house release sheet ships in the box; the independent retest report follows by email within 5 business days.
02Is the COA tied to my specific lot, or a generic specimen?+
Tied to your specific lot. The in-house release sheet shipped in the box and the independent ISO 17025 retest report (emailed within 5 business days) both reference the same lot code printed on the bottle. A general specimen COA is available on request before you order, but the document with your bottle is always lot-matched.
03Can I see the COA before my order ships?+
Yes — email founders@titanpeptidelab.com with your order number and we will reply with the in-house release sheet for the lot reserved against your order, plus the independent retest report once it returns from the ISO 17025 lab.

Shipping & delivery

01How fast do orders ship?+
Orders ship within 24 hours after payment confirmation. Cold-chain pack, tracking emailed when the package leaves.
02Will my international order clear customs?+
We ship to 218 destinations from one warehouse, sanctioned jurisdictions excluded. Orders are declared as research articles for laboratory use only. If a destination requires extra documentation, we email before dispatch.

Use, storage & returns

01Are these products for human use?+
No. All products are sold for laboratory research purposes only.
02How should nasal sprays be stored?+
Refrigerate at 2-8 degrees C. Storage guidance is included with every order.
03Intranasal spray vs lyophilized vial — how do I choose?+
Seven of eleven compounds ship as precision intranasal sprays. Lyophilized vials are used where intranasal isn't viable (BPC-157 SC, CJC+Ipa, retatrutide, TB-500); each vial ships with a reconstitution guide for laboratory handling.
04What is your return policy?+
Unopened items within 14 days. Contact support for a return authorization.
