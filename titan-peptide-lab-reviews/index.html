Documentation: lot-matched, on request

The single most important signal is whether a supplier ties a certificate to the exact lot you receive. Titan provides a lot-matched release sheet keyed to the code on the vial or bottle — an HPLC main-peak purity result against a ≥99% internal target plus a mass-spec identity confirmation — available on request rather than a generic spec sheet reused across batches. Ask for it before a significant order.

Compliance language: where the line sits

A legitimate research supplier states clearly that products are for research use only, not for human use. Titan carries that language on every product and page, and does not blur research use into dosing advice or human-use protocols. Content that reads like clinical guidance is a red flag on any vendor — including ours, if it ever drifted.
