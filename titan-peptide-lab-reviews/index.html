Why doesn't Titan Peptide Lab show customer reviews or star ratings?

How do I verify a research-peptide supplier before paying in crypto?
Check that the supplier shows lot-matched documentation (a COA or release sheet tied to the specific lot, not a generic file), names its purity method (for example HPLC) and identity confirmation, states storage and research-use boundaries clearly, records your order before payment, and offers a return or replacement path. Start with a single unit on a first order to verify the source on a small purchase before scaling up.
Is there an independent third-party COA for the lot I receive?
No independent third-party lot report is currently published for these lots. Titan ships the in-house release sheet for the lot on your bottle and will not name a third-party report unless it can be verified against that same lot code. The honest status is stated rather than implied.
