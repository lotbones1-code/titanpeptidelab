Reconstitution

Reconstituting your Retatrutide

For research preparation only. Use bacteriostatic water (0.9% benzyl alcohol) and a sterile insulin syringe. Reconstituted peptide is stored refrigerated; check the storage guide for stability windows by compound.

  1. STEP 01

    Bring to room temperature

    Let the lyophilized vial and bacteriostatic water sit at room temperature for ~10 min. Cold vials can fracture when injected.

  2. STEP 02

    Inject bac-water slowly

    Draw your chosen bac-water volume into a sterile syringe. Inject down the side of the vial — never directly onto the powder.

  3. STEP 03

    Swirl, do not shake

    Gently swirl until fully dissolved. Solution should be clear. Refrigerate. Discard per the stability window for this peptide.
