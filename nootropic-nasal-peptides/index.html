Nasal format sets concentration, not dose

Nasal research peptides ship lyophilized and are reconstituted before in-vitro work. The diluent volume sets concentration (mg/mL), not the amount of peptide in the vial. Model any volume against vial size before you draw an aliquot — the format does not change that math.
